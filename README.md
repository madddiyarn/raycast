🚀 Raycast

AI system that turns Telegram job messages into real hiring

What it does

Raycast connects to Telegram job chats and converts messy messages into structured vacancies.

Telegram → AI → Job → Match → Apply → Interview

Key Features
AI Telegram bot (understands RU + KZ + slang + translit)
Explainable Match Score (not random)
Candidate profiles (skills, languages, availability)
Work Score (candidate reliability)
Employer Trust Score
ne-click apply
Interview scheduling + AI questions
Vacancy quality & safety checks

Why it’s different
No forms for employers
Telegram-first
AI cleans and structures data
Full hiring flow, not just listings

Vision

Turn chat chaos into a structured hiring system.
